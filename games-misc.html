Background

At Kabam, my primary focus was on developing content for a game called Dragons of Atlantis. I was responsible for creating in-game copy, overseeing content within the Help Center, and developing standard texts for Kabam’s Customer Service team.

When working on in-game copy, I was responsible for improving our onboarding experience and naming new items. Occasionally, I was also responsible for exploring key scenarios and character concepts.

While working on the Help Center, one of my primary goals was increasing our deflection rate for customer tickets. To do this, I worked with our internal team to redesign the Help Center's functionality and created template articles to standardize the structure and quality of our content. In doing this, I was able to help increase the deflection rate on tickets for my game to a company high of 90%.
